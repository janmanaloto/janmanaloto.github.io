hi! Let me introduce myself.

I'm Jan Romeo Manaloto, a Computer Engineer. I have been designing websites for small businesses, friends, and groups ever since I was 14 years old.
Because I was so passionate about creating software I have since then studied and made systems using PHP, Java, jQuery, and node.js.
I also incorporate these languages with Wiring & WiringX to make more intuitive electronic projects and prototypes.

languages I use.

Aside from English, Tagalog, and a little bit of Kapampangan, I also use the languages below. For front-end development, I use HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Bootstrap, and at times, Angularjs. For systems development, Java, JavaFX, PHP, and node.js are what I like to use, paired with MySQL as the database (sometimes I use MongoDB or SQLite).
Wiring or WiringX are used with my electronic projects, using Arduino or Raspberry pi boards. I usually fuse these projects with node.js, java, html etc., because I like accessing everything via web. I also studied Verilog programming, which is used for Integrated Circuits design.
